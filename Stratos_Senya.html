Stratos Unit


"Senya"


Ability:Kinetic Reservoir

power: The hero can absorb kinetic energy from impacts or movement and store it for later use. They could absorb the force of an enemy's punch and redirect it as a devastating attack, or absorb energy from their surroundings (like falling debris or a speeding car) to enhance their own speed or strength Weakness: The hero is vulnerable to being overwhelmed if they absorb too much energy at once. There’s a risk of losing control, and if they don’t release the stored energy periodically, it could become dangerous for both them and their surroundings.
