Biography

The Pillar of Temperance, Amir, Was one of the two people personally Recruited by Shinji, off the streets. Shinji saw Amir, Who was homeless when they first met, being ridiculed by those who passed by Amir. pedestrians called Amir all sorts of names, "Bum", "Tramp", or even used as an example for parents walking with their kids. Amir never said anything back though, Shinji saw this and approached Shinji, offering him some of the finest wine in the city. But Amir declined, he said he didn't want to sully his body with that "Devils Liquid." Shinji saw this as surprising, he was the first to decline such fine wine, so with an extended hand he recruited Amir, Granting him the title the "Pillar of Temperance".​

Powers

The villain’s power is rooted in their endless determination. Their abilities manifest as an unbreakable resolve that allows them to withstand physical, emotional, and mental challenges while forcing their enemies to relent through sheer persistence. Whether by healing themselves continuously, outlasting opponents, or wearing down the will of others, this villain is capable of pushing beyond normal limits, refusing to accept defeat. ​

Weakness

The more the villain relies on their powers, the more they risk overextending themselves. There is a limit to how much life force they can absorb or how much energy they can siphon. If they push too far, they may temporarily exhaust themselves or become vulnerable
