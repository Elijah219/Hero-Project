Amir

Role: Pillar Of Temperance​

Origin: America

Traits: Very Modest and Self Aware

Biography

The Pillar of Temperance, Amir, Was one of the two people personally Recruited by Shinji, off the streets. Shinji saw Amir, Who was homeless when they first met, being ridiculed by those who passed by Amir. pedestrians called Amir all sorts of names, "Bum", "Tramp", or even used as an example for parents walking with their kids. Amir never said anything back though, Shinji saw this and approached Shinji, offering him some of the finest wine in the city. But Amir declined, he said he didn't want to sully his body with that "Devils Liquid." Shinji saw this as surprising, he was the first to decline such fine wine, so with an extended hand he recruited Amir, Granting him the title the "Pillar of Temperance".​

Powers

Has the power to Moderate, Suppress, and regulate the actions, thoughts or emotions of others. ​

Weakness

50 Km range, Fragile Body, Constant Internal Struggle. If the Target Has a strong state of mind the effects of his powers are weakened

Factions

  • Allies: Seraphfall​
  • Rival: Stratos Unit​
