Senya

Role: Stratos Operative​

Origin: Japan

Traits:

Biography

Senya was born into a renown and rich family. He was a prodigy since birth, Cold to everyone, and never had trouble getting anything. In college he decided he wanted to join the Military, Serving 4 years in the Army before moving over to Stratos Unit. Once he passed the entrance tests and Physical Evaluations, he was granted CRX-42 to see if he was compatible with it. When he gained his powers he served stratos unit for about a year before being assigned into a Group with Zachariah, Who he did not get along with all to well. Senya had a Grudge with Zachariah since he scored higher than him on the physical evualuation.​

Powers

The hero can absorb kinetic energy from impacts or movement and store it for later use. They could absorb the force of an enemy's punch and redirect it as a devastating attack, or absorb energy from their surroundings (like falling debris or a speeding car) to enhance their own speed or strength ​

Weakness

The hero is vulnerable to being overwhelmed if they absorb too much energy at once. There’s a risk of losing control, and if they don’t release the stored energy periodically, it could become dangerous for both them and their surroundings

Factions

  • Allies: Stratos Unit​
  • Rival: Seraphfall​
