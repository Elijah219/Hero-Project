Stratos Unit - Overview

Recruitment

The Stratos unit recruits members who apply directly from a position in the army. Once Applied, applicants are put through rigorous training, and for those who make it past the training are given the serum “CRX-42” to gain their superpowers.​

Rankings

#1 Stratos Apex
#2 Stratos Enforcer
#3 Stratos Operative
#4 Stratos recruit

Summary

Serves Directly Under the US Military, Tasked with apprehending or eliminating superpowered creatures/humans. Highly Confidential In their work.​
