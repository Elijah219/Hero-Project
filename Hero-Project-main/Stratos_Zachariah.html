Zachariah

Role: Stratos Recruit

Origin:

Motivate:

Biography

Zachariah was always the responsible type, being a big brother of two little siblings honed him into the overly cautious and calculated person he is. He decided to join the Air force after high school, wanting to serve his country and protect those who lived in it. After 5 years in the Air force, He heard of a branch called Stratos unit, he decided to apply for stratos unit. The entrance exams and training camps were gruesome to him, but he kept the image of his siblings in his mind, motivating him. Once the final day for camp was here, The scores of the physical evualtions and iq tests were posted, he scored #1 on the Physical and #7 on the iq tests, these scores allowed him to get a hold of CRX-42. Ultimately, he was compatible with CRX-42 and after a year of serving stratos unit he was put into a group called Team Delta with Senya and a veteran that served stratos unit for 8 years.​

Powers

Event Horizon - The user can generate a localized “event horizon” - a boundary in space beyond which nothing, not even light, can escape. This manifests as a dark, spherical zone around the user or at a targeted location. Anything crossing this zone experiences extreme gravitational distortion, time dilation, or is pulled into a pocket dimension or singularity.

Weakness

Control Limitations: Creating an event horizon is extremely taxing; prolonged use may harm the user.
