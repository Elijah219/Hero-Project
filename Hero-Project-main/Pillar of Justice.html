Pillar of Justice / Shinji

Role: Leader of Seraphfall​

Origin: Emberreach

Motivation: Giving reward and punishment according to the merits of the case, impartially and consistently

Biography

Originally in the Stratos Unit, The pillar of Justice, Originally known as Shinji. Broke away from Stratos after seeing them has "corrupt" for hiding CRX-42 from the world, He thought everyone should have a chance to have these new, superpowers. This mindset lead him to create Seraphfall, where he planned to Achieve the dream of a world where everyone had powers.​

Powers

The villain wields ethereal, ever–balancing scales that weigh the moral worth of every soul nearby in real time. No deed—benevolent gesture or petty thought—goes unnoticed. The moment the scales detect even the slightest imbalance (a pang of guilt, a selfish whisper, a suppressed doubt), bellowing “gavel strikes” echo through the battlefield and sentencing verdicts are delivered in merciless force. ​ Within a 50-foot radius, every combatant’s heart and mind are scanned. Visible, spectral chains lash out at anyone whose emotional “balance” tilts, however minimally, toward imperfection.​ ​

Abilities

  • Sentencing Chains: Deals necrotic damage proportional to the target’s moral “weight” (even heroism can carry arrogance.)​ Remorse Shackles: Temporarily binds victims, immobilizing them while inflicting psychic pain equal to the emotional “guilt” they carry from past actions (or imagined failings).​
  • “Purity Purge” for typically “good” characters: strips away buffs, boons, healing—“you are too pure; your righteousness blinds you.”​ Neutrals: inflicts ongoing psychic bleed, as if dredging up every hidden doubt. Villains: curses their abilities, turning their own powers against them.​
  • Inferno Surge

Factions

  • Allies: Seraphfall​
  • Rival: Stratos Unit​
