Biography

The Pillar of Charity, Akane, was a generous person ever since she was young, she made sure everyone was included in school activities and more, a true people pleaser at heart. One day, one of her Childhood friends, Rin. Asked her to go to this odd gathering with her. Akane, of course said yes, so when Akane sat down with rin in this abandoned building with seats all surrounding the middle where the light from the city peered through, she was a bit on edge, But hearing the words of the Seraphfall preachers talk about how powers are being kept from the public and should be allowed to everyone, she found herself siding with Seraphfall more and more. As the gather proceeded, she saw the preachers call her friend Rin up to the middle, injecting her with this serum they called CRX-42. Her eyes widened as her friend rin, experienced now enhanced strength, she wanted this strength as well. Once the Ceromony ended, she walked up to the preacher, asking for CRX-42 herself so she could join, the preachers gladly obliged, injecting her, and once she found out she was also compatible with the serum, she decided to join Seraphfall as well.

Powers

By “giving” of themselves, the villain transfers their life force and strength to allies who’s pledged to the villain, siphoning her own vitality to fuel an unstoppable army of the charity-bound. The villain harms himself to increase strength in allies ​

Weakness

The Pillar has to sacrifice herself to increase strength in others, sacrificing her own body.
