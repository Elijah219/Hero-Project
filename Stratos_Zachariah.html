Stratos Unit


"Zachariah"


Ability:Event Horizon

power: Event Horizon - The user can generate a localized “event horizon” — a boundary in space beyond which nothing, not even light, can escape. This manifests as a dark, spherical zone around the user or at a targeted location. Anything crossing this zone experiences extreme gravitational distortion, time dilation, or is pulled into a pocket dimension or singularity. Weakness: Control Limitations: Creating an event horizon is extremely taxing; prolonged use may harm the user. Friendly Fire Risk: Anything within the radius — friend or foe — is affected unless the user has mastered fine control. Cooldown Time: After collapsing the horizon, the user must wait before creating another.
